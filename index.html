About Saarthi

Welcome to Saarthi: One App, Full Power!

Saarthi was developed as part of a **hackathon project** to create a seamless, integrated productivity and utility dashboard. Our goal was to bring together essential daily tools into a single, intuitive interface, reducing digital clutter and enhancing user efficiency.

Our Vision

In today's fast-paced world, managing various aspects of your daily life shouldn't require jumping between countless applications. Saarthi aims to be your trusted "guide" (which is what 'Saarthi' means!), simplifying your digital experience so you can focus on what truly matters.

Key Features Include:

  • **Personalized Greetings & Daily Quotes:** Start your day with inspiration.
  • **Real-time Weather Updates:** Always be prepared for what's outside.
  • **Robust To-Do List & Habit Tracker:** Organize tasks and build positive routines.
  • **Versatile Timer & Stopwatch:** Manage your time efficiently for work or focus.
  • **Advanced Calculator & Unit Converter:** Quick computations and conversions at your fingertips.
  • **Number System Converter:** Seamlessly switch between binary, decimal, and hexadecimal.
  • **Toggleable Dark/Light Mode:** Customize your viewing experience.
